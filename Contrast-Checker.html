Contrast Ratio Information

The contrast ratio measures the difference in luminance between foreground and background colors. Higher ratios (minimum 4.5:1 for normal text) ensure better readability for users with visual impairments.

SpectraCheck helps you create accessible designs by testing color contrast according to WCAG 2.1 guidelines. For complete accessibility testing, consider multiple factors beyond color contrast.
